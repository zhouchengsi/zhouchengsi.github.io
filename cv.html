Research experience

Experimental investigation on Passive Heat Removal System (PHRS) in nuclear power plant

The Passive Heat Removal System (PHRS) is used to removal heat through the steam generators when the auxiliary feed water system has failed. This investigation focus on heat transfer characteristic of the heat removal exchanger and the characteristic of the system pressure drop and nature convection. The calculation model has been established to predict the outlet temperature and the condensation flow pattern inside of the tube.

2011 - 2012

Numerical and Experimental investigation on heat transfer characteristic of hot-wind box in paper industry

This is my undergraduate thesis. The main purpose of the project is to optimize the design of hot-wind box and boost the efficiency of paper drying. The experimental apparatus has been set up to obtain the actual time of drying of different hot-wind boxes. The numerical simulation is carried out by using commercial software, Fluent.
